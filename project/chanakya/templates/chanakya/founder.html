Founder's Vision



For us, education is an umbrella term which has a wide range of purposeful pursuits and we, at chankaya, focus on them with great diligence.

We always wish to groom our students into global citizens and we know that academics solely cannot lead us in the race. The school, therefore, aims at holistic development with a firm belief that every child deserves a vibrant & creative environment to explore his dormant potential.

I believe that Chanakya has now become a landmark which delivers world class education embedded with all the facilities that a child needs for his/her grooming.
