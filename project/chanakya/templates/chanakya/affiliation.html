Affiliation



Chanakya Vidya Mandir is a recognized institution affiliated with the Rajasthan Board of Secondary Education. We are proud to offer the Rajasthan Board's Class 10 curriculum to our students, providing them with a rigorous and comprehensive education that prepares them for the challenges of higher education and the workforce. Our dedicated faculty members are highly qualified and experienced, and they work closely with students to ensure that they have the support and resources they need to succeed academically. We follow the guidelines and regulations set by the Rajasthan Board, and we are committed to upholding the high standards of education set by the Board. Our students have consistently achieved excellent results in the Class 10 Rajasthan Board exams, and we are proud to be a part of their academic journey.
